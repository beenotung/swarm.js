Guideline

  1. Copy to clipboard: Ctrl+C
  2. Switch to your website
  3. Hit F12 or
  4. Right-click -> Inspect
  5. Click Console
  6. Paste to console: Ctrl+V
  7. Hit Enter
  8. Hit F12 again to close the console or
  9. Click the cross at button WITHIN the dev panel
